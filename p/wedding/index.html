Our Story

Our relationship began in October of 2012. A windy walk downtown turned into a drive along Old Mission Penisula to the lighthouse for our first date. Little did we know, about 4 years later we'd take that same drive and become engaged on the shores of the peninsula. We've shared many memories together the past 5 years and grown both as a couple and individuals. We'd be honored if you'd be apart of our wedding day and share a great memory with us in Leelanau County.
